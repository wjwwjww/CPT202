Fitness

Fitness Plan

Silver

Silver Tier (Entry-Level):

  • Access to standard gym facilities and group classes.
  • Limited number of personal training sessions.
  • Basic online support and fitness tracking.
  • Community support through forums or social media groups.

Gold

Gold Tier (Mid-Level):

  • Additional personal training sessions.
  • Nutritional guidance and meal planning.
  • Access to specialty classes or premium gym equipment.
  • Enhanced online support and app features.
  • Participation in fitness challenges and events.

Diamond

Diamond Tier (Mid-Level):

  • Additional personal training sessions.
  • Nutritional guidance and meal planning.
  • Access to specialty classes or premium gym equipment.
  • Enhanced online support and app features.
  • Participation in fitness challenges and events.
